# Habit-Helper

Our team of 3 built a prototype of a wireless, wearable device which aids the user in correcting poor posture and low physical activity. Accelerometer data are collected and processed by an Arduino Nano microcontroller to calculate statistics and provide helpful reminders to the user that are sent wirelessly (Bluetooth) to our smartphone application. Our smartphone application shows users' health results and notify them of their condition with regards to posture and physical activity. Furthermore, a vibrating mini motor disc was integrated to act as a notification system when the user is slouching for some time. Images of our prototype, its block diagram, schematic, phone application, and flowcharts of our device and smartphone application can be seen below. Feel free to take a look at the Final Report document for more information about our prototype!
